Miles Studio has integrated Perforce support, with other popular SCC solutions to come.

To enable source control, configure the perforce login information in the Options tab and click "Commit". Notice there is no entry for password - Miles Studio assumes that you have logged in to Perforce elsewhere (via P4V/P4Win/etc).

Once set up correctly, each bank must be enabled separately. Under each bank's Properties, check "Under Source Control".

An enabled bank attempts to sync the Perforce state for each asset when the asset is saved. Meaning:

  • If the asset is not detected in Perforce, it will be added.
  • If the asset is in Perforce, it will be checked out for edit.
  • If the asset has been deleted in Miles Studio, it will be marked for deletion in Perforce when the bank that contained it is saved.
  • Renames do not use inherent Perforce renaming capability - the old asset will be marked for deletion, and the new one marked for add.
