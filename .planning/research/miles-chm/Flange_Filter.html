This filter is also used to to produce a swirling effect on a sound. The filter delays a copy of the sample data, but uses a low frequency oscillator to vary the speed of the copy's playback. (The oscillator moves in the range of 1-20 cycles per seconds to get the effect.) Feeding the processed signal back into the device to be processed again can get a more intense effect.

Name for use with AIL_find_filter: "Flange Filter"

Properties:

"Flange Feedback": Regeneration amount. Range: -0.9 to +0.9

"Flange SweepRate": Rate of delay change. Range: 0.01 to 10.0 cycles per second.

"Flange Depth": Delay of flange signal. Range: 1.0 to 1000.0 milliseconds.

"Wet Level": Volume level of the processed data. Range: 0.0 (no effect) to 1.0 (full effect).

"Dry Level": Volume level of the original data. Range: 0.0 (no effect) to 1.0 (full volume).

When a mix value of 0.0 is specified, the filter will operate on an internal "fast path" designed to consume minimal CPU resources.
