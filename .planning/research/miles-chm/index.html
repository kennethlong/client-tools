Welcome!

Welcome to the Miles Sound System!

We're happy to release this new version of the Miles Sound System. Miles 9 supports several new platforms and runtime features, but the big news is the complete rewrite of the Miles Sound Studio! This new authoring tool is more flexible, friendly and powerful!

Your sound designers can now author completely in parallel with the game, debugging and auditioning audio events directly in your game (even with hot-loaded sounds and events). And the entire system is source-code controlled and deploys easily to multiple platforms and languages, so your programmers will love it too.

Again, please send feedback to me as you use Miles - what confused you, what you liked, what you hated, what you'd like to see in future versions, etc. Your feedback is what keeps Miles vital, fun to use, and powerful after all these years!

Thanks for all your support,
