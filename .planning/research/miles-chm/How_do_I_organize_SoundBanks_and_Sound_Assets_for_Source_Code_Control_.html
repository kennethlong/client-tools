Q: How do I organize SoundBanks and Sound Assets for Source Code Control?

A: Sound banks and their assets should be checked in to source control, and Miles Studio can manage this for you. If you have your Perforce settings correct in the Options tab, then click on the name of the bank in the Asset List, and check "Use Source Control". Miles Studio will then always try to keep Perforce up to date with what you are doing.
