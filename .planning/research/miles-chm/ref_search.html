Allows for searching of strings in assets. Any string field in an asset is searched.

  1. The string to search for. Wildcards work (? and *)
  2. The name of an asset with a match.
  3. What part of the asset caused the match.

Fairly straightforward. This is just a string search - nothing really fancy. You can use wildcards. Results specify where in the asset the match occurred, as with complex assets it can get a little difficult to spot where the match was.
