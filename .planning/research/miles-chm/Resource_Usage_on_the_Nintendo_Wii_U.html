We have tried very hard to be as low-impact on the Wii-U hardware as possible.

As with all platforms:

  • We use a background thread for sound mixing and timer servicing.

  • A background thread manages all IO, and FSAReadFile alignment is handled transparently.

We've worked hard to make MSS as easy to drop in as any of our other platforms - unlike a lot of other middleware, we think you'll be pleasantly surprised at how little work you'll have to do.
