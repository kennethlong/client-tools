For digital output, the Miles Sound System uses the OpenAL. Miles allocates a single sound buffer, and then mixes all of its sound channels into that single buffer.

Miles for GNU/Linux uses its built-in DLS synthesizer for all MIDI playback. This has the added advantage of allowing you to author custom DLS instrument sets to optimize memory use.
