Consoles generally require means of recovering from disc errors. This is explained in more detail in the eventstreamerror example, but the general idea is to watch your streams for the error wherever you do your sound enumeration, and requeue the event that started the stream once the disc has been replaced. When you enqueue, pass in the current stream location via AIL_stream_position through the user buffer in some manner.

When the replacement stream is in the Pending state, you can call AIL_set_sound_start_offset with the value of AIL_stream_position at the time of failure to cause the stream to restart where it left off.
