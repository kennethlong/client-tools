Like the other next-gen systems, the PS3 is a powerful machine and includes all of the conveniences of modern operating systems. The trickiest part of using the PS3 is making good use of the SPU - Miles not only makes great use of these super-fast little computation units, it also does so in a way that is almost completely hands free!
