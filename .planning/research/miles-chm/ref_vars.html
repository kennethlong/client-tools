As of version 9.1, Miles Studio requires variables be defined prior to usage. Usually, a variable's value is controlled entirely by the game, and the sound designer's job is just to route them in interesting ways. However, a variable's default value can be assigned, as well as a basic LFO. If you desire more control over the variable's value over time, the Move Variable event step is the tool available.

When setting a variable to LFO, the controls are as you would expect. The only thing to know is the Frequency is capped by nyquist - meaning it can't move faster than half your framerate (this will be addressed in future releases). As a result, avoid any oscillations faster than around 15 Hz.
