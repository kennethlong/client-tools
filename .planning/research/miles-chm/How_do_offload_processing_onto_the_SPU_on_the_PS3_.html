Q: How do offload processing onto the SPU on the PS3?

A: Miles makes using the SPUs very easy - the actual sound APIs are identical!

  1. Call MilesStartAsyncThread to install the SPU image onto the SPU.
  2. Use the SPU flag that matches the spu index that you passed to MilesStartAsyncThread.

Now, Miles will use that SPU image to do almost all of its processing (mixing, resampling, decompression, DSP filters and more). About the only major feature not there is Ogg Vorbis decompression.
