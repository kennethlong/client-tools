The Miles Sound System on Wii-U supports the same uncompressed PCM, IMA ADPCM, Bink Audio, MP3, and Ogg Vorbis digital sound formats that we support on our other platforms. ADPCM, MP3 and Ogg Vorbis are not natively supported by the Wii-U, but Miles makes the decompression and streaming to the sound hardware seamless.

Miles for Wii-U also offers fully-integrated 2D and 3D sound.

Miles for Wii-U uses its built-in DLS synthesizer for all MIDI playback. This has the advantage of allowing you to author custom DLS instrument sets to optimize memory use.

Miles for Wii-U also supports playing audio on the DRC. You can either open a device that only outputs to the DRC, or you can open an eight channel device where the final two channels are directed to the DRC. To do this, see MilesWiiUDRC.cpp for an example.
