HSAMPLE AIL_allocate_bus(HDIGDRIVER dig);
Description

Allocates a bus to mix samples to.

Parameters
dig [in]

The HDIGDRIVER to allocate the bus on.
Return value
return [out]

The HSAMPLE for the new bus.

Discussion

A bus allows you to treat a group of samples as one sample. With the bus sample you can do almost all of the things you can do with a normal sample handle. The only exception is you can't adjust the playback rate of the sample.

Use AIL_bus_sample_handle to get the HSAMPLE associated with a bus.

Each call to AIL_allocate_bus adds a new bus, up to a total bus count of MAX_BUSSES. After the first call, two busses exist - the main bus and the first aux bus. The HSAMPLE returned is for the first aux bus (index 1)
