vcxproj edit, and one SetupClientGraphics one-liner that wires install() into
the engine bootstrap.

- CSV writer state (StdioFile open/append/close, row format per RESEARCH.md interfaces)

- ExitChain teardown that closes any open CSV and unregisters the DebugFlag

clean even though no callers exist yet — that's expected within-phase ordering.

| disk write → host filesystem | StdioFile abstraction; written to a fixed subdirectory `<exe-dir>/dpvs-profile/` (config-key override available) |
