Resolve the scaffold_fatal_stub FATAL surfaced by Plan 11-09.9's smoke: StarAppearance::LocalShaderPrimitive::draw -> Graphics::setPointSize hits a Plan 11-02 STUB at Direct3d11.cpp:617. Same applies to the other 5 point-sprite setters at lines 618-622.

Diagnosis (Plan 11-09.9 close smoke):
- FATAL c57b5047 "Direct3d11 plugin: scaffold-only -- unimplemented Gl_api slot called (Plan 11-02 expected; Wave 3+ replaces this)"
- Stack: StarAppearance::draw (StarAppearance.cpp:337) -> Graphics::setPointSize (Graphics.cpp:832 -- `NOT_NULL(ms_api->setPointSize); ms_api->setPointSize(size);`) -> scaffold_fatal_stub.
- Direct3d11.cpp:617-622: all 6 point-sprite slots (setPointSize/Max/Min/ScaleEnable/ScaleFactor/SpriteEnable) routed via `STUB(slot)` reinterpret_cast to scaffold_fatal_stub.
- Direct3d11.cpp:613-616 comment block already states the design intent: "D3D11 has no fixed-function point-sprite control; gl_PointSize-equivalent lives in HLSL via SV_PointSize. Plan 11-06 stubs out the setters as no-ops; Plan 11-07 smoke surfaces whether engine particle subsystems rely on engine-side point-sprite emit." The comment was correct about D3D11 semantics; the STUB macro was the wrong mechanism (it FATALs instead of no-oping).

Fix approach:
- Add 6 inline no-op impl functions in the existing Direct3d11Namespace anonymous-namespace block (or wherever the other `_impl` no-op helpers live in Direct3d11.cpp).
- Replace the 6 `STUB(setPoint...)` lines at Direct3d11.cpp:617-622 with `ms_glApi.setPoint... = setPoint..._impl;` assignments.
- Update the surrounding comment to reflect the realized intent (Plan 11-09.10 lands the no-ops).

Visual side-effect: stars draw at 1-pixel hardware-default size on D3D11 (vs 2-pixel point sprites on D3D9). Acceptable for Iter-1 — fidelity deferred to Plan 11-11 visual-parity if it matters in practice. SV_POINTSIZE doesn't work at vs_4_0 / FL10+ (only at FL9.x via deprecation path); real point sprites would require GS-side quad expansion, which is a separate plan.

Scope ceiling: ONE iteration. Pure function-pointer reassignment + 6 trivial no-op definitions. Zero structural risk.

The D3D9 plugin doesn't need this change — its setPointSize/etc. wire to real D3DRS_POINTSIZE / D3DRS_POINTSPRITEENABLE / D3DRS_POINTSCALE* render states (Direct3d9.cpp:2303-2342). D-05 invariant maintained.
